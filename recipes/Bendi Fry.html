Bendi Fry

Description

Bhendi Fry (alternately called Okra fry, Bhindi fry, Bhindi masala or Bharwan bhindi) is stir fried okra (ladies' finger) that is slit and stuffed with spice mix such as garam masala and other locally available ground spices. This dish is stir-fried or saut√©ed slightly, which is distinct from batter-fried okra, which involves deep frying. It can be served as a side dish in accompaniment with steamed rice and dal or with chapati as a form of sabzi.

Ingredients

  • Ladies Finger - 15 chopped into thin rounds
  • Oil - 2 tblspn + 1 tblspn
  • Mustard Seeds / Kaduku - 1 tsp
  • Onion - 1 chopped finely
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
  • Dried fruits
  • Coriander powder - 2 tsp
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. First rinse 250 grams okra (ladies finger) very well in running water a few times. Then drain them completely in a strainer or colander.
  2. Wipe each okra with a kitchen napkin. There should be no moisture on them. You can also allow them to dry naturally at room temperature, by spreading them on a large plate or tray.
